Our Story

Born from Passion,
Brewed with Purpose.

KŌFEE was founded by Arjun Mehta in 2018 after a transformative journey through Ethiopia's Yirgacheffe valleys. He witnessed firsthand how coffee farmers poured generations of knowledge into every harvest — and returned home determined to honour that craft.

Today, KŌFEE works directly with 14 family farms across 6 countries, paying above fair-trade prices and co-designing sustainable farming practices. Every cup you drink is a direct link to the hands that grew it.

Farm to Cup

The Soil Makes
the Difference.

We travel to origin every harvest season — cupping hundreds of lots before selecting only those that meet our quality threshold. Altitude, soil composition, processing method, and the farmer's philosophy all matter deeply to us.
